# Design Brief: Vedic Astrology Section

## Objective
Create a dedicated, highly authentic, and visually stunning section/page for Vedic Astrology services. The design must feel "Sacred & Authentic" (non-AI/digital vibe) to build trust with users seeking spiritual guidance. It should emphasize the accuracy gained by combining Janm Kundli, Prashna Kundli, and Gochar.

## Target Audience
Individuals seeking serious life guidance regarding career, marriage, business, and health, who value traditional wisdom and practical remedies.

## Aesthetic Direction: "Sacred & Authentic"
- **Style**: Traditional Indian aesthetic blended with modern clean UI.
- **Vibe**: Scholarly, ancient, trustworthy, and spiritually grounded.
- **Avoid**: Neon colors, robotic/futuristic elements, generic "space" gradients, flat "app-like" icons.
- **Memorable Element**: A central "Triple-Pillar" or "Trinity" visual representing the combination of Janm, Prashna, and Gochar Kundli.

## Content Structure
1. **Hero Header**: "VEDIC ASTROLOGY: Accurate Life Guidance through Vedic Astrology". Use a mix of English and Hindi (ज्योतिष शास्त्र).
2. **Introduction**: Brief explanation of how Vedic Astrology works (Janm Kundli + Prashna Kundli + Gochar).
3. **The Three Pillars (Deep Analysis)**:
   - **Janm Kundli (Birth Chart)**: Life pattern, Career, Marriage, Health, Finance.
   - **Prashna Kundli (Question-Based)**: Specific question instant answers.
   - **Gochar Kundli (Transit)**: Current planet positions and upcoming time.
4. **Targeted Solutions**: Icons/Cards for Career Confusion, Marriage Delay, Business Problems, etc.
5. **The Deliverables**: What the user receives (Personalised prediction, Clear guidance, Practical remedies, Confidentiality).
6. **Call to Action (CTA)**:
   - Primary: [Book Kundli Analysis]
   - Secondary: [Talk on WhatsApp]

## Typography
- **Headings**: Elegant Serif (e.g., Playfair Display, Lora) to convey tradition.
- **Body**: Clean, readable Sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat).

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Saffron (#FF9933) or Ochre (#CC7722).
- **Background**: Very dark charcoal or deep Maroon (#1a0f0f) with subtle parchment or stone textures.
- **Accents**: Gold (#D4AF37) and Cream (#F5F5DC).

## Technical Requirements
- **Framework**: Next.js (App Router).
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS.
- **Components**: Use existing Shadcn UI components where appropriate but customize them heavily for the theme.
- **Icons**: Use Lucide-react icons but styled with a "hand-drawn" or "etched" look (stroke width, color).
- **Output**: Create a new component `components/VedicAstrology.tsx` and a page `app/vedic-astrology/page.tsx`.

## Image Needs
- A central "Vedic Mandala" or "Zodiac Wheel" that looks authentic/traditional.
- Background texture (subtle paper/parchment).
