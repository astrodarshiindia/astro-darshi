# Design Brief: Astromall Gemstone Store

## Objective
Create a high-end, high-converting Gemstone Store page (Astromall) for a Vedic Astrology website. The page should inspire trust and project a premium image.

## Target Audience
Individuals seeking astrological remedies through gemstones, valuing authenticity, certification, and spiritual alignment.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Overall Style**: "Non-AI", professional, premium, and modern spiritual.
- **Color Palette**: Deep blues (primary), gold accents (luxury), clean whites (purity).
- **Typography**: 
  - Headings: Serif font (`Cormorant Garamond` / `--font-cormorant`).
  - Body: Sans-serif (`Geist` / `--font-geist-sans`).
- **Composition**: Elegant spacing, clear hierarchy, high-quality imagery (placeholders/generated).

## Content Structure
1. **Hero Section**: 
   - Title: "Get Right Gemstone Based on Your Kundli"
   - Highlights: "Lab Certified Stones", "Proper Recommendation", "Energization Process"
   - Visual: Premium gemstones floating or a professional arrangement.
2. **Why Gemstones (Scientific + Astrological Logic)**:
   - Content: Explain resonance with planetary frequencies vs superficial fashion.
3. **Certification & Trust**:
   - Visuals: "Lab Certified Proof", mentions of GRS, GIA, or IGI.
   - Highlight: "Energization (Abhimantrit)" process.
4. **Product Categories**:
   - Categories: Pukhraj (Yellow Sapphire), Neelam (Blue Sapphire), Manik (Ruby), Panna (Emerald), Moti (Pearl), Moonga (Red Coral).
   - Presentation: Elegant grid or scroller with high-quality thumbnails.
5. **Key Features / CTA**:
   - "Consult before you buy"
   - "Upload Kundli & Get Suggestion"
   - "Customization: Rings & Pendants"
   - "Payment: COD & Prepaid options"

## Technical Requirements
- **Framework**: Next.js 15+ (App Router).
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4.0.
- **Icons**: Lucide React.
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first, fully responsive.
- **Components**: Use existing `Header` and `Footer` components.
- **Animations**: Use Tailwind transitions or `tw-animate-css` for subtle entry effects.
- **Output Path**: `app/astromall/page.tsx`.
- **Additional Files**: Create necessary sub-components in `components/astromall/` if needed.

## Image Needs
- Premium-looking gemstones (Sapphire, Ruby, Emerald, etc.).
- Macro shots of stones reflecting light.
- Certification seal icon/graphic.
- Spiritual/Vedic motifs for background subtle textures.
- **Format**: Prefer raster (WebP/PNG) for stones, SVG for simple icons.
